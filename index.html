About Sunset Escape

Escape to paradise at Sunset Escape Villa, a luxurious six-bedroom retreat nestled in the serene landscapes of Homagama, near Colombo. Our stunning property perfectly combines modern elegance with tropical charm, offering an unforgettable experience for families, large groups, and special events. Beyond our spacious bedrooms and pristine private pool, we provide premium entertainment, featuring a dedicated Karaoke Room and a Pool Table housed in a comfortable, air-conditioned space.

Whether you're planning a weekend getaway, a family reunion, or a special celebration, our villa provides the perfect backdrop with its spacious interiors, pristine pool, and lush gardens. Experience comfort, privacy, and luxury all in one beautiful location.

With premium amenities, dedicated service, and attention to detail, we ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary. Book your escape today and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Booking Terms & Policies

Booking Policy

  • Advance Booking: Minimum 7 days advance booking required for Night Out packages. Day Outing bookings accepted 3 days in advance.
  • Payment: 50% deposit required at booking confirmation. Balance payable upon arrival.
  • Check-in/Out: Night Out check-in: 2:00 PM, check-out: 11:00 AM. Day Outing: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM.
  • Maximum Capacity: Maximum 15 guests for Night Out. Minimum 10 guests for Day Outing package.
  • Identification: Valid ID proof required for all guests at check-in.

Cancellation Policy

  • 7+ Days Before: Full refund of deposit minus 10% processing fee.
  • 3-7 Days Before: 50% refund of deposit amount.
  • Less than 3 Days: No refund on deposit. Balance amount refunded if applicable.
  • No Show: Full payment forfeited, no refund.
  • Early Departure: No refund for unused nights/hours.
  • Force Majeure: Full refund or rescheduling option in case of natural disasters or government restrictions.

House Rules

  • Parties: House parties and loud music allowed until 10:00 PM only.

  • Safety: Guests must follow all safety instructions, especially around pool area.
